Cheesecake recipe

ingredients

  • 3 slices of bread (white, whole wheat, or multigrain)
  • 2-3 slices of cooked turkey or chicken breast
  • 2-3 slices of ham
  • 2-3 slices of bacon, cooked until crispy
  • 2-3 slices of tomato
  • 2-3 leaves of lettuce
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Toothpicks (for serving)

Instructions

Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
Spread a thin layer of mayonnaise on one side of each slice of bread. If desired, spread a thin layer of mustard on top of the mayonnaise.
On the first slice of bread, place the lettuce, tomato slices, and turkey or chicken breast. Season with salt and pepper.
Place the second slice of bread on top, mayonnaise side down. On top of this slice, place the ham, bacon, and cheese.
Top with the final slice of bread, mayonnaise side down.
Secure the sandwich with toothpicks and cut it into quarters for easier serving.
