Lemonade recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 4-5 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lemon slices (for garnish)
  • Fresh mint leaves (for garnish, optional)

Instructions

    In a small saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and the granulated sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved. Remove from heat and let the syrup cool.
    In a pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice and the cooled syrup.
    Add 3-4 cups of cold water to the pitcher and stir well. Taste and adjust the sweetness or tartness by adding more water or lemon juice as desired.
    Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
    Serve the lemonade over ice cubes and garnish with lemon slices and fresh mint leaves if desired.
